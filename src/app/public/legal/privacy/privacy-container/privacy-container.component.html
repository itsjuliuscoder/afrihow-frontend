1. Information we collect, how we collect it, and why

1.1 Information You Provide to us
Our visitors

You can use our platform even without you being a registered user, but we automatically store information such as your location, browser and system information as cookies for us to offer better services whenever you use our platform.

Registration

For you to have better experience using our platform, you will have to create an account which will require you to provide data that includes your fullname, email address and a password.

Profile

After creating an account, you can decide to update your profile by providing an image and a short description about yourself. Having a complete profile helps us and other users of afrihow.com know you better.

Subject

You are required to create a subject which will enable users on our platform to learn from, this subject may be a business, organization, education institution, etc. You will provide data that includes subject name, description, website url, image and a category. This will enable us understand the subject you are creating.

Topic

To enable users on our platform to start learning about your subject, you will be required to create a topic which will request for data like topic title, description and tags.

Creating Article

After going through the process of creating a subject and a topic, you will have to create an article for an already created topic. This article will be shared live on afrihow.com

1.2 How we collect information

Our main method of getting your information is directly from you and we can as well automatically get it through the use of our Services.

Information Gotten From You

Typically, the information we collect directly from you are from our registration form details and our email subscription lists, profile update form, subject and topic creation form. You decide how much information to share with us in most cases, but not sharing required information may limit your ability to engage in certain activities, such as creating of subject, topic or article on our public platform

1.3 Why we collect your information

The main reason we collect your information is for you to have better experience using our platform, both for our vistors and registered users.

Online Vistors

Anytime you visit our platform, we automatically collect your information such as your location, browser and system information for us to serve you better whenever you use our platform.

Registered Users

For you to have better experience using our platform, you will have to be a registered user. This will enable us to better organize your learning experience, you will be able to subscribe to subjects of your interests, follow topics and read articles in a well organised format. You can as well, share what new to learn about and build your learning network
